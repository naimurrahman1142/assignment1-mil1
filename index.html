Our Donate Plan

All that we do at Plan Bangladesh is underpinned by our Values.

Food for a family

Most children in Bangladesh do not have access to proper meals. Through our healthy food initiative, we provide a balanced meal to then to ensure that they carry on their lives well.

Education for a child

We aim to ensure that all the children gain access to equitable education through providing necessary resources and training of teachers to provide quality education

Shelter for a family

Lack of better healthcare is a problem in Bangladeshi children. As an organization, we have established links with local healthcare givers to provide medical help to rural families

We Are Nonprofit Team

This is a nonprofit organization located in Redwood City, CA. We are dedicated to empowering nonprofit leaders through continued learning and to building spaces where everyone has a seat at the table and individuals are comfortable bringing their whole self.
